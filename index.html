About Me

I'm a PhD student at Cornell University advised by Greg Morrisett and Alexandra Silva. Previously, I was a master's student at the University of Pennsylvania working with Stephanie Weirich. Before that, I was a senior software engineer at Strava. My research has been supported by Cornell's Dean's Excellence and Hopper-Dean Fellowship as well as the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship. You can check out the information below to get an idea of what I've been up to recently, or see my CV.

Research

My research interests are in the theory and semantics of programming languages. Broadly, my goal is to develop tools and techniques that help avoid subtle bugs and unintended behavior in complex programs. You can check out this page for a brief summary of past and current projects.
