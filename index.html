About me

I was born and raised in Puerto Rico, where friends and family usually call me Emma. You're more than welcome to do the same! I'm currently a master's student at Penn. I've been fortunate to work with Prof. Stephanie Weirich, who has been an incredible mentor. Together, we've written a paper in literate Agda about logical relations and studied effects and coeffects in call-by-push-value.

On a personal level, I have many interests: cats, running, video games, climbing, cycling, food. I'm also passionate about diversity, equity, and inclusion; I hope to contribute to building safe spaces and increasing participation in research and academia. I've benefitted greatly from the help and advice of other students, and am happy to pay it forward. If you're going through the grad school application process, feel free to reach out!
