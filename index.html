About me

I was born and raised in Puerto Rico, where friends and family usually call me Emma. You're more than welcome to do the same!

This coming fall, I'll be starting a PhD at Cornell. I'm currently a research intern at the University of Minnesota, working with Favonia. Previously, I was a master's student at Penn. I was fortunate to work with Stephanie Weirich, who is an incredible mentor.

On a personal level, I have many interests: cats, running, video games, climbing, cycling, food. I'm also passionate about diversity, equity, and inclusion; I hope to contribute to building safe spaces and increasing participation in research and academia.
